Simple Vegetarian Recipes

Paneer Butter Masala

Description: A creamy dish with soft paneer cubes simmered in rich tomato-based gravy.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Butter, cream, spices

Preparation: Make a smooth masala with onion, tomato, spices, and butter. Add paneer and simmer.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

Veggie Pasta

Description: Tasty pasta tossed with colorful veggies, herbs, and olive oil.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Bell peppers
  • Tomato sauce
  • Oregano, cheese

Preparation: Boil pasta, sauté vegetables, add sauce and cheese, mix well.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Vegetable Biryani

Description: Spiced rice dish cooked with mixed vegetables and aromatic herbs.

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Carrot, beans, peas
  • Biryani masala
  • Yogurt, fried onions

Preparation: Cook rice and vegetables, layer with masala and yogurt, steam and serve.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆
